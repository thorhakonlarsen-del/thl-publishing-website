The Evidence-Based Nutrition Coaching Playbook

Systems, Scripts, and Science for the Modern Nutrition CoachSystemer, skript og vitenskap for den moderne ernæringscoachen

Your clients have the macros. They have the meal plan. They still ghost you at month three. The problem isn’t the science — it’s the system. This book gives you what certification courses leave out: adherence frameworks, complete coaching scripts, and scalable SOPs that cut per-client time from 90 minutes to 25.Klientene dine har makroene. De har kostplanen. De forsvinner likevel etter tre måneder. Problemet er ikke vitenskapen — det er systemet. About the BookOm boken

The Evidence-Based Nutrition Coaching Playbook is the first complete practice manual for certified nutrition coaches. It delivers adherence frameworks grounded in self-determination theory, motivational interviewing scripts for common client situations, a 6-step plateau protocol, scalable SOPs that cut per-client admin time by 40–60%, and a clear roadmap from 15 to 150 clients. Written by a coach with 10+ years of experience and the builder of the Veraleva AI coaching platform, every recommendation carries an evidence grade (GREEN / YELLOW / RED).

The Evidence-Based Nutrition Coaching Playbook er den første komplette praksismanualen for sertifiserte ernæringscoacher. Den leverer adherence-rammeverk basert på selvbestemmelsesteorien, MI-skript for vanlige klientsituasjoner, en 6-stegs platåprotokoll, skalerbare SOPer som kutter administrasjonstid per klient med 40–60 %, og en tydelig vei fra 15 til 150 klienter. Skrevet av en coach med 10+ års erfaring og utvikleren bak Veraleva AI coaching-plattform. Hver anbefaling har en evidensgrad (GRØNN / GUL / RØD).

Who This Book Is ForHvem boken er for

For certified nutrition coaches, registered dietitians, and personal trainers who offer nutrition coaching. Whether you have 10 clients or 100, this book provides the systems infrastructure to grow your practice sustainably. Also valuable for gym owners and coaching businesses building team-wide SOPs.

For sertifiserte ernæringscoacher, autoriserte ernæringsfysiologer og personlige trenere som tilbyr ernæringscoaching. Enten du har 10 eller 100 klienter, gir denne boken deg systeminfrastrukturen for å vokse bærekraftig. Også verdifull for treningsentereiere og coachingbedrifter som bygger teambaserte SOPer.
