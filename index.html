About the Author

Thor-Håkon Larsen

Thor-Håkon Larsen is a Norwegian author, economist, and entrepreneur whose work sits at the intersection of business strategy, human performance, and emerging technology. Drawing on a career built across finance, operational management, and digital ventures, he writes with the rare combination of analytical rigour and narrative clarity.

His non-fiction titles — spanning productivity, expertise, and artificial intelligence — have earned praise for their practical depth and uncompromising focus on results. In parallel, his speculative fiction trilogy Den delte verden explores a near-future Norway grappling with algorithmic authority and the quiet dissolution of human agency.

Larsen believes that the best books do one thing: they change how you think. Everything published under the T.H. Larsen imprint is held to that single standard.
